Welcome to Pocket Science Lab @ FOSSASIA.

The Pocket Science Lab project at FOSSASIA develops open source hardware and software technologies to improve science education by changing the way we teach and learn. We enable citizen scientists around the world. PSL will deliver low-cost lab equipment to millions of students and young scientists. We revolutionize science by making it accessible for everyone to to experiment and explore the universe. PSL is Free Software and Open Hardware and is inspired by ExpEYES and MicroHope.
