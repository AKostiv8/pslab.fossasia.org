Welcome to Pocket Science Lab @ FOSSASIA.

Pocket Science Lab aims to develope open source hardware and software technology to improve science education by changing the way we teach and learn. PSL aims to deliever low-cost lab equipments to millions of students and young scientists and enable them to learn science by experimenting and exploring.

PSL will be using Free Software and Open Hardware frameworks like ExpEYES (www.expeyes.in), MicroHope (www.microhope.org) and other open source tools.
